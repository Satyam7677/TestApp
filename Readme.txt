How to launch the app?
- To launch the app clone the project and ryn command yarn, then yarn pods
- After that run yarn ios or yarn android to start app in simulator or emulator

App structure
- App is divided into 3 modules
  1. Auth
  2. Home
  3. Settings
  
How to use?
- Sign in to the app using any data{username and password} from 'https://dummyjson.com/users'
- After signing in you will be navigated to Home screen
- A list of products will be displayed
- Search the product and click on that product to get detailed information.

How to log out?
- To logout get to the settings tab and click on logout.
